## Small sized Learning Management System at Private Academy, 2017
    1) Customer (Student) contact history database.
    2) Quiz sites (Vocabulary, Reading, etc.).
    3) Payment & Credit (Point) management system.

An SI project where I aimed to build a comprehensive system for service business management. I started with the education sector and learned about LMS (Learning Management Systems). Unfortunately, the contract ended before I could fully realize the vision. 

[Korean]
